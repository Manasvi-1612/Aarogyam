We are aarogyam

Welcome to our mental health website, where we strive to provide a safe and supportive space for individuals seeking information, resources, and support for their mental health. Our mission is to break the stigma surrounding mental health and promote mental wellness through education, advocacy, and community. Our team of mental health professionals and advocates are dedicated to providing accurate and up-to-date information on mental health conditions, treatment options, and self-care practices.
